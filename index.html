How mid™ works

Shortly after you subscribe you'll be invited to a private trello board where you can make your first design request. A few days later you'll have a design in hand. And don't worry, if it's not perfect, we'll revise it until it is. Once you've approved, we'll move on to the next request.

Why not hire a full-time designer?

Hiring a full-time designer not only requires a large time investment but also a financial one. The average designer's salary can start around $200k.
with mid™ you and your team get instant access to an experienced designer, allowing your team to make progress as quickly as the first week you subscribe.
 even better, you can simply pause your subscription at any time if your design needs slow down. you can't do that with a full-time designer.

how many requests I can have?

You and your team can add as many backlog requests as you'd like to the queue. Based on your prioritization they will be designed and delivered one by one.

How fast will I receive my designs?

On average, for most requests you'll receive a first draft in just three days or less. However, more complex requests can take longer — but don't worry we'll let you know if that's the case.

why do you use an async process?

In order to focus on maximum quality deliverables and stay in flow, we work completely asynchronously. We've found that meetings erode from the amount of work we can produce, and we're all about the work.

How do I manage my subscription?

Your trello board will have a link to your customer login where you can pause or cancel your subscription. If your design need dries up, simply pause and you can come back whenever to use the rest of the month you've already paid for.

What design software do you use?

We use Figma.

How do I request designs?

simply move a trello card with all the details of your request into the 'active request' column. feel free to attach a google doc, wireframes, sketches, or even recording a quick Loom video. we'll comment if there is any clarification needed and then we'll jump into the work.

Are there any refunds?

Sadly, no. Due to the amount of work we put in to ensure you the best results in a short period of time, we can't justify going uncompensated for our time and effort.
 But if you're unsatisfied for any reason with our service, please let us know and we'll do our best to help.
